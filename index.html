PICKLBALL

PLAY

ABOUT

Three mini-games to prime your hands,
eyes, and mind before you hit the court.

DRIVE
A rapid-fire reaction game. Tiles light up one at a time across a grid — tap the active tile before the timer expires. Miss the tile or tap the wrong one and it's over. Hits build a combo multiplier up to ×16, rewarding streaks over a 30-second run. Hard mode cuts the reaction window nearly in half.
↳ Fires up hand-eye coordination and sharpens split-second decision-making — the same reflexes you need when a fast drive is coming at your feet.
DROP
A memory sequence game. The board flashes a pattern of tiles in order — watch, remember, then reproduce it exactly. Sequences grow longer each round, and a single wrong tap ends the game. Easy mode gives you more time to study the pattern; hard mode blinks through it fast.
↳ Activates working memory and spatial recall — the same mental focus needed to read the court and anticipate where the next ball is headed.
DINK
A rhythm and timing game. Tiles scroll downward in columns — tap the correct column when the active tile reaches the bottom row. The pace accelerates as your score climbs. Hard mode blacks out the board, leaving only a floating tile visible so you're navigating almost purely on timing.
↳ Trains rhythmic timing and sustained focus under pressure — exactly the patience and precision the dink game demands at the kitchen line.
